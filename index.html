About LEY

LEY (Legal Education for Youth) empowers high school students—especially those in underserved communities—with practical legal knowledge. We believe every young person deserves to understand their rights and the laws that affect their everyday lives.

We provide in-school workshops, peer-led legal education, and viral digital content to make law accessible, engaging, and empowering for youth.

Programs

  • Know Your Rights Workshops: Interactive lessons delivered in high schools and community centers, covering essential legal topics like police encounters, tenant rights, and youth employment laws.
  • Social Media Legal Series: Short videos and explainers that teach legal concepts through TikTok and Instagram in a relatable way.
  • Youth Legal Ambassadors: A leadership program training high school students to educate their peers on rights and justice issues.
