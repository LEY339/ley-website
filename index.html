About Us

Legal Education for Youth was founded to ensure that teens everywhere could be educated on their rights and understanding legal education further. It was built for youth that are interested in pursuing a career in law, people who want to understand why and how their society was built and functions, and teens that want to be able to confidently know their rights during pressing matters.
